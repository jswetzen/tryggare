Features

Everything a busy Sunday needs

Designed for the reality of church life — multiple volunteers, fast check-in lines, and parents who want to feel confident.

Multi-station check-in

Any number of staff can check children in or out simultaneously from any browser on the local network.

Real-time sync

WebSocket broadcasts keep every station instantly up to date — no refreshing, no delays.

Label printing

Automatic label printing via Brother QL printers with a full print queue and job tracking.

QR code lookup

Each child gets a unique QR token. Public QR pages require no login — parents can check in from the door.

Full audit log

Every check-in and check-out is attributed to a staff member with an exact timestamp.

Data import

Import families directly from FestivalPro and Planning Center — no manual data entry to get started.

Self-hosted

Your data stays yours

  • No per-child fees, ever Run it on your own hardware. The only cost is your server.
  • No cloud dependency Works entirely on your local network. No internet required on check-in day.
  • One-command deploy Docker Compose setup. Works on Coolify, a Raspberry Pi, or any VPS.
  • Built on proven open-source Django + SvelteKit + PostgreSQL. No lock-in, no proprietary stack.
